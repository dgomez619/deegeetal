These four images were taken in different countries. They all have a special meaning and they transport me to that moment in time. One of them is a surfing competition, the other photos are a sunset in Central America and in the Indian Ocean, also a trip to Big Bear mountains with my mother. To organize these images, I have used the Grid System. They are laid out as a photo collage, which gives the site a modern look. The images resize automatically so they'll always fit the viewport properly, I added a shadow effect to give it a fun touch and modern feeling to my site.
