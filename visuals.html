In this section of my Web Site I have implemented an image slider, with the help of some awesome tricks of CSS I was able to design the slider and add 5 images that are from different trips that I have made recently. The images are organized neatly and it has an interactive way of switching images, there is a scroll bar at the bottom of the slider that can be dragged left or right, there's also a numeration on top that can take you from image number 1 to image number 5 in just one click.
