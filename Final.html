This four images were taken in different countries, they all have a special meaning and they transport me to that moment in time, one of them is a surf competition, the other photos are a sunset in Central America and in the Indian Ocean, also a trip to big bear mountains with my mother. To organized this images I have used the Grid System, they are layed out as an photo collage, which gives the site a modern look, the images resize automatically so they'll always fit the viewport properly, I added a shadow effect to give it a fun touch and modern feeling to my site
